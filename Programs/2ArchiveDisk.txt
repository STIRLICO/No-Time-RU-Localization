--This is a copy from Archive Disk #12--

2023 GLOBAL ENTRY
-Asian Alliance has been formally established
-Eastern forces take over and divide the Republic of China.

2024
-Israel is surrounded by war

2027
-Military ships are in a standoff on the mediterranian sea

2028 GLOBAL ENTRY
-The Pope visits Moscow
-Czechia was annexed by Russia.
 
2028 Global Entry Late Autumn
-The murder of a high ranking official in the Bucharest Conference marks the beginning of the war.
-The United States of Columbia are suprised by the sudden declaration of war by Russia though they believe it will take time for the east to muster forces

2028  October 28th
-A sudden attack of the East from Prague on the West and Europe. Defending forces flee.
-Three divisions roll over Germany over Night, it all goes fast. Highways are unsafe, people escape over the country side.
-The three military columns, 15 divisions each, advance and go all the way to the Rhine River, where they get halted. No bridge stands.

2028 November 10th
-A swarm of drones fly from the southern deserts over Prague all the way to the Baltic Sea.
-They drop boxes containing highly radiated waste material which falls like yellowish smoke in one line from Prague to the Baltic Bay.
-None of the invaders can now cross the line, either to escape or to move forward.
-Anyone who crosses falls dead instantly. Flesh turns dark and falls of bones, even the bugs in the earth die.

2029 GLOBAL ENTRY - January 1st
-Silvester Night, a sudden attack on the island chain of Acoria
-St. André destroyed by rockets.

2029 February 10th
-War continues. AREA 1 was taken by resistance forces. Mr. Westwood was killed by an Agent. Only few robot warriors remain.
-A nuke lands in the North Sea, creating a tidal wave which sinks all of South England under water, even parts of Germany up to the regions of Berlin and Hamburg.
-Germany gets invaded over night by three battalions from the east in Prague.
-Asian Forces try to invade over Alaska

2029 February 15th
-Paris was leveled with the ground by revolution and lastly nuclear fire. 

2029
-February 17th an asteroid passes close to the earth, one part of it goes short and burns up in the atmosphere, pieces of it destroy the country side of East Germany.
-Earthquakes all over the world. Cracks are opening up, deeper than the Grand Canyon.

2029 February 19th
-A darkness covers the sky, thunder claps, people still hide underground.
-A cross is seen in the sky, people are afraid.
-The Great War ends abruptly as the world stands still.

2031 GLOBAL ENTRY
-Outbreak of the burning plague, whoever dissolved the hawthorn leaf in boiling water and took it was helped. -People applied it three times a day.

2032 GLOBAL ENTRY
-A local lord retakes great parts of France.
-After capturing france, the lord who claims to be heir to Louis XVI, moves his french forces to Italy to recapture Rome.

2033 Friday April 15th
-Previous nights have been dark and especially cold
-Wind howls like the roar of lions
-On the dawn of friday no sun rises, no light can illuminate the darkness
-People cling to blessed candles to make light
-If someone tries to go out, they die on the spot

2033 Sunday April 17th
-A great morning, the sunlight and climate is different, warmer. All together there were three days of darkness.
-Southern fruits grow in the northern hemisphere
-Sea Levels are lower

2033 Sunday April 24th
-The french Prince gets crowned by the new pope. King Henry V of the Cross, Emperor to the New Holy Roman Empire.
-Edward R. Athor makes a journey to france, to visit the new king.

2034 August 15th 10:00
-Edward R. Athor gets elected Mayor over what is left of Pine Lake and holds a speech.
-People from all corners of Acoria, including St. André flock to Pine Lake to start anew.
-Pine City is founded.
-The west of the island gets transformed into a hub of water harvesting.

2035
-Pine Island joins the NHRE (New Holy Roman Empire).

2038 August 15th
-Mayor Edward R. Athor runs for a second term.
-King Henry visits Pine City and speaks with the people.

2042
-Mayor Edward R. Athor runs for a third and last term.

2046
-Mayor Edward R. Athor gives over his title of Mayor and makes a last journey to europe, to spend his last years at the emperors palace. His friend Jason Connor gets elected Mayor in his stead.
