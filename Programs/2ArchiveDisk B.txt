March 28, 2088
 Edward Athor is seriously ill.
-Mayor Connor and his companions travel to France to visit the King and Edward.

April 11, 2088
-Edward R. Athor dies in his bed in the royal gardens, surrounded by his friends and family. He was 131 years old.

2090 World Entry 
-The so-called Long Peace begins

2101
-In honor of Mayor Edward Athor's magnificent life, a statue of his likeness is erected on the Plaza level in Pine City.
-Human life expectancy increases two- to four-fold.

2121
-Moon City is officially open to human settlers. The number of settlers will rise to over a thousand in the next fifty years.

2200
-Eurasia experienced a large influx of new settlers. Thanks to temperate climates worldwide, the barren expanses of Siberia now appear as a vast, barren paradise.

2259
–The Pine City spaceport opens to the public and becomes Acoria's hub for travel to orbital stations and the moon.

2300
–Pine City extends across the entire northern part of the island.

2499
–Atha Corp. is founded in Zhongguo Province.
