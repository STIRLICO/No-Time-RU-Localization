--This is a copy from Archive Disk #1216--

2659
-People protesting in front of the Atha Headquarters, Zhongguo Province. To make an end to human cloning.

2665
-The emperor is declaring sanctions on the Atha Headquarters as Cloning Technology gets more and more widespread. A new law was introduced by the imperial senate, banning the use of human cloning. Much to the dismay of many voices. Protests in Zhongguo Province continue.
-Several heads of the Atha Group were arrested by imperial guards.

2696 - World Entry - The first cloner storm
-Atha clone masters assault The NHRE. The north african borders were overtaken quickly, Varangia Capital is under siege. The emperor has sent his finest imperial guardians in the defense of the capital.
-Strange readings have returned from the suns surface. Local probes read possible signs of degradation in sun light intensity.

2705
-Pine Island has become the focal point of the war on Acoria. Imperial guardians retake the city.

2761 - World Entry - Second Cloner Storm
-With cloning technology rapidly increasing, it now takes mere handful of years to grow clone armies compared to a normal human life time.

2795
-Terrorist cloner sympathisants claim responsibility to have leveled Moon City with a fission bomb. The casualites count to half a million. 
-Chunks of the moon reenter the surface, damaging many more stations of the moon by debree.

2799
-The eleventh crusade [Clone Crusade] has been launched by the holy father with the sole purpose of destroying cloning technology.

2802
-Clone armies retake the Pine City a second time, though the old city center is heavily contested by Imperial Guardians!

2805
-Siege of the Atha Warforge [Main HQ], Zhongguo Province. The already mentally unstable clones go mad all over the world due to the growth inducers.

2806
-Siege of the Atha Warforge, Zhongguo Province continues. The empire slowly regains the upper hand.

2821
-The cloners have been defeated, 125 years of a destructive and terrible conflict.

2822
-The sun is dying.
-Many citizen from Acoria and other lands migrate towards the equator for a warmer life with more sunlight.

2900
-Is Acoria including Pine City gets more abandoned.

2969
-King Henry dies as oldest man in the world during his journey and ascension to the Mount of Olives in Jerusalem.

Post 2970
-Fall of the Holy Roman Empire. Arrival of the Lawless. In these last days the courage of men failed, forsaking all friendship and breaking all bonds of fellowship.
Is any hope or faith left in the world?
